Five steps. Every engagement, every fee level.

01

Briefing

A structured conversation about your objective, your timeline, your constraints, and the decision you are trying to make. Every briefing receives a personal, considered response within one working day.

02

Diagnosis and matching

A written recommendation: the disciplines your situation calls for, the specialists we propose, and a plain language description of what the engagement will produce. You decide.

03

Working session

A structured session with the matched expert. Within 48 hours you receive a written brief: the real situation beneath the presenting problem, what must happen in what order, and the shape, timeline, and cost of the full engagement.

04

The engagement

Deliverables, milestones, timeline, and fee agreed in writing before work begins. All work runs through one channel with a named Aximo owner. Fees attach to defined outcomes, never to open ended hours.

05

Outcome report

A written close: what the situation was before, what specifically changed, and what the next 90 days should focus on.

One axis. Seven engines. Momentum.

Aximo is an advisory and execution firm based in Nairobi, serving companies and investors that are entering, expanding, or growing in East Africa. We take our clients from decision to result through one accountable relationship: one firm, one process, one standard, and written proof that the work delivered what it promised.

The name.

Aximo is drawn from the word axis: the central line around which everything turns. It describes exactly how we work. Seven engines of expertise, from marketing to law to market entry, turn around one axis, which is a single accountable relationship with the client. And an axis in motion generates momentum, which is the outcome every client is actually buying. A business that was stuck starts moving. A market entry that would have taken a year takes a quarter. Capital that was waiting gets deployed.

The problem we solve.

East Africa is one of the fastest growing investment destinations on the continent, and Kenya is its commercial hub. Capital and companies are arriving. What has been missing is the execution infrastructure to receive them.

A company entering the region must coordinate lawyers, accountants, recruiters, banks, regulators, and agencies in a market it does not yet know. The expertise exists, but it is fragmented: priced for multinationals at the top end, unverifiable at the bottom end, and accountable to nobody once the advice is delivered. Projects stall, costs multiply, and capital stays on the sidelines.

Aximo replaces that fragmentation with one relationship. We diagnose the situation, assemble the right expertise, run the work through a single structured process, and answer for the outcome.

Our mission.

To give companies and investors operating in East Africa a single accountable partner that turns decisions into results: one relationship, one process, one standard, and written proof that the work delivered what it promised.

Our vision.

To be the firm of record for market entry and business growth in East Africa. When a company anywhere in the world decides to enter the region, or an investor decides to deploy capital here, Aximo is the first call.
